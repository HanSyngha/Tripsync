TripSync Account Deletion

How to delete your account and data

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly in the app:

  1. Open the TripSync app
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Type "DELETE" to confirm
  6. Tap the Delete Account button to complete

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Trip itineraries you created
  • Chat messages
  • Trip participation history
  • App settings and preferences
âš ï¸ Important Notice
  • Account deletion is irreversible
  • If you are a host of any trips, please transfer host privileges or delete the trips first
  • All data will be completely deleted within 30 days after the deletion request

Data Retention Period

  • Immediate deletion: Profile information, app settings
  • Deleted after 30 days: Chat messages, trip data (removed from backups)
  • Retained as required by law: Payment records and other legally required information may be retained according to applicable laws
